About Us

Tout Plus Savoureux, located in downtown Chicago, blends Parisian elegance with the city's vibrancy. Founded by renowned French chef Pierre Dubois, it offers a refined atmosphere and exceptional French cuisine. Chef Dubois combines traditional techniques with modern innovations, crafting dishes that are culinary works of art. The menu highlights classic French flavors with fresh, seasonal ingredients. The restaurant's wine list, curated by a sommelier, ensures perfect pairings. Recognized with Michelin stars and other accolades, Tout Plus Savoureux is a top destination for memorable dining experiences, offering impeccable service and a warm, sophisticated ambiance.
